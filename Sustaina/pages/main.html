To enhance the environmental sustainability at Ohio State University (OSU), we can implement a multifaceted approach. This includes investing in renewable energy sources, adopting energy-efficient building practices, and promoting recycling initiatives. OSU can further encourage sustainable transportation methods like biking, public transit, and carpooling. By incorporating environmental education into the curriculum and fostering a culture of sustainability through student-led organizations, OSU can make a lasting impact in reducing its carbon footprint and promoting eco-friendly practices throughout the campus community.
